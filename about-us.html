About LingArch

 LingArch is a global leader in legal, certified, and sworn translation services. We specialize in delivering accurate, industry-specific translations in 120+ languages, helping law firms, corporate clients, and individuals communicate across borders with confidence.

We were founded in the year 2000 and have grown rapidly and organically over the past two decades as a translation agency.

Why Choose LingArch?

LingArch combines expert linguists, industry-specific knowledge, and cutting-edge technology to deliver fast, accurate, and reliable translations tailored to your needs.
